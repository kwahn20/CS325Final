Overview

The Marquis at the Regatta is on the 7th floor with beautiful full on views of the gulf and the bay. Only a 5 minute walk from the highly regarded Vanderbilt Beach, this property has everything you need to enjoy the Western Coast of Florida to the greatest extent. If you're seeking downtime, you can enjoy the free WiFi, Apple TV, and the large flat screen television in the living room. You can soak up the breeze and watch the sunset over the ocean from the freshly furnished, screened in deck. Gorgeous new hickory hardwood floors, a temperature controlled wine fridge, and nespresso machine are just a few more luxuries you can find at the Marquis.
